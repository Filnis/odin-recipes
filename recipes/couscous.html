Cous Cous

Description

Delicious and healthy lunch, ideal for when you don't have a lot of time

Ingredients

  • Cous Cous
  • Dried tomatoes
  • Hard cheese
  • Zucchini

Procedure

  1. Dice the Zucchini
  2. Dice the cheese
  3. Put the cous cous in a pot with a volume of water equal to the volume of the cous cous
  4. Heat the pot until all the water has boiled away
  5. Mix the cous cous with the cheese, zucchini and dried tomatoes
